    <h1>Kui</h1>

    <p><i>Dravidian > South-Central > Gondi-Kui > Kuvi-Kui > Kui</i></p>

    <p>Kui is a tribal Dravidian language spoken mainly in the Indian state of Odisha. Due to being situated in a predominantly Indo-European speaking state, it has adopted some Indo-European vocabulary, as well as the Odia script.</p>
